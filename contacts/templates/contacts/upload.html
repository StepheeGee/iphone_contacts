What is a VCF File?

VCF (Virtual Contact File), also known as vCard, is a standard format for storing contact information including names, phone numbers, emails, and more. These files are commonly used by smartphones and email platforms for contact backups and transfers.

Why Convert to Excel?

Excel (.xlsx) format makes it easy to view, edit, and organize your contacts. Whether you're moving to a new device, creating a contact list for work, or managing your address book, converting to Excel helps you stay organized.
